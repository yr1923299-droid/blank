Who is Bhagwan Vishnu ?

Lord Vishnu is one of the principal deities of Hinduism and the Preserver in the Trimurti. His role is to maintain cosmic order and restore balance whenever dharma is threatened.His wife is Goddess Lakshami and son is Kamadev.

Avatars

Matsya
The fish-man avatar of Lord Vishnu to save Vedas and humans from the great tsunami.

Kurma
The tortoise avatar of Lord Vishnu for supporting the Mandar Parvat in Manthan.

Varah
The boar-man avatar of Lord Vishnu to save the Earth from the deep of cosmic ocean(Garbodhak Mahasagar) and killing the Hiranyakashyap.

Narsimha
The lion-man avatar to save his devotee Prahlad and world from the bad deeds of the Overconfident Hiranyakashyapu.

Vaman
Came as a short Brahmin to measure three world(lokas) in one after becoming big in size.

Parshuram
The aggressive avatar of Lord Vishnu who freed world from bad(paapi) kshatriyas 21 times.

Ram
The gentle and purusottam avatar of Lord Vishnu to defeat evil demon ten-headed Ravan and rescuing Sita mata from the Lanka and giving world a important lesson.

Krishna
The speaker of Geeta and cute-naughty avatar in Vrindavan of Lord Vishnu and the leader of Arjun and pandavs in Mahabhart.

Buddha
Most calm avatar of Lord Vishnu .He established the buddhism and preached his teaching of life related to Moksha.

Kalki
The future extensively strong avatar to be taken to establishDharma again and killing The Kalipurush...
